Practice all programs in the examples section before solving exercise programs.

1. MyAddress : write a program to print your address in console.
Ex: O/P =>
Full Name,
S/D/O XYZ,
AAAAA village,
BBBBBBBBB,
PinCode-123456

2. BigLetter : write a program to print your initial letter as below.
Ex: O/P => My Initial is M
MM       MM
M M     M M
M  M   M  M
M   M M   M 
M    M    M
M         M

3. FriendName : write a program to print your name and your friend name in different lines using two print statements (Don't use println statements).
